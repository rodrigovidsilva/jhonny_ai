# First Outreach Message

## Short Version

Hi {{name}},

We are building a business copilot for small retailers. It connects to your POS and stock system, then gives you live dashboards and lets you ask questions like:

- How much did we sell today?
- What is our current stock value?
- Which categories are selling?
- What products are low in stock?

The app is working locally against live retail data, and the same backend can support WhatsApp so the owner can ask questions without opening a reporting tool.

We are running a small number of paid pilots with retailers. Would it be useful to show you a 10-minute demo using a real retail store example?

## Follow-Up

Hi {{name}},

Quick follow-up. The goal is not another dashboard project. The goal is to give owners instant answers from the systems they already use.

The first pilot focuses only on sales, stock, purchases, and owner questions. WhatsApp can be added when the retailer wants that channel. If it does not save reporting time or improve stock decisions, it is not worth continuing.

Open to a short demo this week?

## Discovery Call Questions

1. What do you use for POS and stock?
2. How often do you check sales and inventory reports?
3. What questions do you ask your team every week?
4. Do you know how much money is currently tied in stock?
5. Would WhatsApp access be useful for the owner or store manager?

## Demo Note

Current demos should be run local-first from the Next.js app at `http://127.0.0.1:3000`. Do not promise a hosted tenant until cloud access and deployment scope are confirmed.
